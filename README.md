# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard-

Evaluate Sales Performance: Identify total revenue and transaction volumes across different store locations (e.g., Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, Lower Manhattan).

Identify Peak Trends: Determine peak sales hours and high-traffic days of the week to optimize staffing and operations.

Analyze Product Mix: Discover top-performing product categories (Coffee, Tea, Bakery) and specific product details to inform inventory and marketing strategies.

Customer Insights: Understand spending patterns based on product sizes and unit prices to refine pricing strategies.
